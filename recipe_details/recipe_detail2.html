Quick Fudge Brownies

1 Reviews

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: American

Meal: Snack

Diet: Gluten-Free

Total time: 1 hr 15 mins

Prep time: 40 mins

Cooking time: 35 mins

600

300

Ingredients

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 cup unsalted butter

1 large egg

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 cup unsalted butter

Serves: 3

Directions

Step 1:

1. Heat an oven safe (preferably cast iron!) 12-inch skillet over low heat.

2. Let the skillet heat up for 5 minutes.

3. Add the butter and let it melt completely.

4. Stir in the sugars with a wooden spoon.

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins

Step 2:

1. Whisk together the egg and egg yolks in a bowl.

2. Add it to the skillet sugar mixture and mix in right away with a spoon, quickly mixing so the eggs don't cook from the heat.

3. Stir in the vanilla extract.

4. Stir in the dry ingredients until just combined. This will take a minute or two and the mixture will be thick, but they will mix in! You can wipe the top of the skillet or the sides with a damp paper towel to remove excess flour.

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 0 mins

Step 3:

1. Stir in the chocolate chips.

2. Bake the skillet cook for 20 to 25 minutes, or until it’s mostly set in the center.

3. Top with flaked sea salt.

4. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 25 mins
